Communication & Interaction

Users can communicate through various means, including text, images, videos, and sometimes audio. Features such as comments, likes, shares, and direct messaging enable interaction between users.

News and Information

Many people use social media as a source of news and information, following pages, accounts, or groups related to their interests. Platforms may curate content based on user preferences and engagement.

Business & Marketing

Social media is widely used for business and marketing purposes. Companies and individuals leverage these platforms to promote products, services, or personal brands. Advertising and sponsored content are common features on many social media platforms.
